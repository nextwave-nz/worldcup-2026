Delete Your Account & Data

Applies to the Road to Glory app (package kiwi.fifawc.app) and fifawc.kiwi

You can ask us to delete your account and all data associated with it at any time. Here's how, and exactly what gets removed.

How to request deletion

Email us from the address you signed in with:

To: hello@nextwave.nz
Subject: Delete my account

Sending from your signed-in email lets us verify the request is yours. If you used a different email, just tell us which one you registered with.

Email a deletion request

What gets deleted

We permanently remove:

  • Your account record and the email address linked to it
  • Your predictions and prediction history
  • Your leaderboard entry (display name, points, stats)
  • Your memberships in any private leagues, and any leagues you created

How long it takes

We process deletion requests within 30 days. Once complete, the data is gone from our systems and cannot be recovered. We don't retain copies afterward, except anything we're legally required to keep (typically none for this app).

If you never signed in

The app works without an account. If you never signed in with an email, there's no account on our servers tied to your identity — your predictions live under an anonymous ID. To remove that local data, simply clear the app's storage in your device settings or uninstall the app.

Prefer to just stop?

You can sign out any time from the app's More tab. Signing out leaves your data intact in case you return; use the email request above if you want it permanently deleted.

Contact

Any questions about deletion? Email hello@nextwave.nz.
