Delete Your Account & Data

Applies to the Matchday NZ app

You can delete your account and all data associated with it at any time. The fastest way is right inside the app - it's immediate and complete.

Delete in the app (instant)

  1. 1. Open the app and go to the More tab (you must be signed in)
  2. 2. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete my account"
  3. 3. Confirm, and your account and data are deleted immediately

Or request deletion by email

No longer have the app installed? Email hello@nextwave.nz with the subject "Delete my account", from the email address you signed in with (so we can verify it's you). We process email requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Both methods permanently remove:

  • Your account record and the email address linked to it
  • Your predictions and prediction history
  • Your leaderboard entry (display name, points, stats)
  • Your memberships in private leagues, and any leagues you created

Deleted data cannot be recovered, and we don't retain copies afterward.

If you never signed in

The app works without an account. If you never signed in with an email, there's no account on our servers tied to your identity - your predictions live under an anonymous ID. To remove that local data, simply clear the app's storage in your device settings or uninstall the app.

Prefer to just stop?

You can sign out any time from the app's More tab. Signing out leaves your data intact in case you return; deletion is for when you want it gone for good.
